Our Initiatives

Making Computer Science For All

Education

We run workshops, school visits, and outreaches with youth around the nation where students teach students.

Manned by our member chapters and curricula, we introduce the foundations of digital literacy, tech, design, and more. Students can opt into intensives and explore project-building through our innovation program.
Youth leave with the groundwork - and most importantly community - they need to succeed in tech.

Making Technology built by all

Innovation

Developh is empowering the youth to develop, and growing the student founder community while we're at it. We run an accelerator on the basis of technology for social good.

Students enter their own venture ideas at any stage, or sign up to work with tech-enabled projects partnered with NGOs. We assist with mentorship, spaces, capital - while students can focus on learning, building, and impacting without sacrificing their schooling.

Cipher is Developh's official women and gender minorities arm. We're working to diversify the technology scene with more representation. Cipher empowers with talks and outreaches, career and mentorship opportunities, and a space for conversation.

DevEd

Short for "Development Education", DevEd is our open collective of educational resources and materials on different technology and creation concepts, completely free to use and access and made entirely by students, who know how students learn best. From Computer Science to CSS to more creative concepts to concrete how-to's, we currently post materials on the DevEd Facebook page. Website coming soon! Visit the DevEd page

About Developh

Here at Developh, we're leading initiatives that seek to redefine the technology sphere through our pillars of education, community, and advocacy. We're gathering the world's largest open tech community while bringing students to build together for advocacy, and connecting creation by hosting events and working on resources and tools that make technology accessible. As we're a nonprofit organization run by students, we're taking all the steps we can to code and create with everyone in hand.
